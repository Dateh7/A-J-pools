Our Story

A-J Pools began with one goal to bring affordable, lasting pool restoration to every home and hotel in Cape Town.

We treat every pool like it is our own

A Local Business Built on Trust and Skill

Eight years ago I had my own swimming pool renovated and it cost a fortune. That experience made me realize there had to be a better and more affordable way to restore pools without compromising quality. I decided to do something about it.

I gathered a skilled and trustworthy team and together we launched A-J Pools with a mission to bring new life to old swimming pools at a fair price. We specialize in refibre lining and fibreglass restoration transforming worn-out gunite and fibreglass pools into beautiful and durable spaces again.

Our process is detailed. We fix every crack and hole, reinforce the structure, and apply premium fibreglass linings that deliver a brand-new look and feel.

What truly sets us apart is our people. My team is honest, respectful, humble, and deeply committed to doing every job right. We have earned the trust of many clients who value our professionalism and reliability.

Let us help you fall in love with your pool again without breaking the bank.
